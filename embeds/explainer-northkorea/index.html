North Korea

North Korea's nuclear progress in 2017

Missiles

North Korea’s efforts to develop a nuclear-armed intercontinental ballistic missile capable of hitting the US mainland have accelerated with a series of successful missile tests, including North Korea’s first launch of an ICBM on 4 July.

Targeting the US

The potential range of the Hwasong-14 tested on 28 July includes the continental US. Observers noted the missile had been quickly modified from its previous test on 4 July – a strong indicator of the regime’s enhanced capabilities.

Nuclear tests

North Korea needs to produce a miniaturised nuclear bomb that can be loaded on to an ICBM to complete a credible weapon. An underground test on 3 September suggested that milestone may be within reach.
