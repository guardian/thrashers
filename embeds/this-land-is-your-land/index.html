This Land is Your Land

America's 640m acres of public lands are under threat. This series on the future of our mountains, parks, rivers and trails is funded by the generosity of Guardian readers, who helped us raise $114,302 to cover the story. For the rest of 2017, we plan to aggressively report on proposed rollbacks of environmental protections and the push to sell off national land.
